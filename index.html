Learner Mentor Collaboration¶

About¶

Its a group of industry experts working in various professional domains (open source and academics only); these domains have a never-seen-before destitution of skilled work force. We pick skilled professionals from these domains to form a teaching group. We learn together as a team and focus on each participant's career development using a data analytics algorithm. Our mission is to provide quality professionals to the industry; and from the industry. And we mean it.

We meet regularly like a fun group. One of us professionals presents his lecture and provides assignments. Our secret recipe is to design these lectures. Learning is a process¶

Learning process goes like a multilevel hierarchy of information gathering. You got to manage and organize your learnings. You also got to be able to look up your own knowledge store when you need to apply a certain skill of yours in the real practical scenario at hand. The web application we are developing has few ideas that we need to test while developing this collaborative learning platform. The ideas are: 1. Use Fibonacci patterns to visualize the learnt material: As we understand from scientific researches that nature follows golden ratio. Some sophisticated plants have been identified to show growth pattern that goes in congruence with golden ratio (this is directly related to fibonacci sequence). The idea is to add new information in a chunk that is in a golden ratio with existing information that we are building upon. On top of that the knowledge, although abstract, got to do something with tangibles; the real world. In order to look up your knowledge base, it should be intuitive to you to browse through your knowledge like you breathe effortlessly. This effortless look up would only come if you could associate your abstract knowledge with examples from your own life experiences. 2. Practice the learnt material using spatial repetition technique: Practice is another equally important aspect of learning. With practice you make your memory stronger and stronger. One researched technique which is related to good memory is spatial repetition. The idea is to challenge the analytical skills of a learner to retreive and apply the knowledge into practical problem domains. Personalized quizes and application of knowledge in hypothetical scenarios. 3. Creativity and inspiration: A learner must be kept inspired to learn. Attributes of inspiration are: a) Showcase tangible applications of abstract skills of peers and mentors. b) Career development, monetory benefits and sense of achievement. c) Involvement, support and drive of a strong mentor community. To inspire the learners and mentors we need to showcase contributions by the peers. May be associate scores with contributions to quantify the learning and mentoring efforts.An individual learning page looks like this

The Blogs (as shown by the bottommost activity in the diagram above) are used to generate Quizes. Quizes can be collected from the internet by contributers. Another way how this can work is to begin with problems to be solved, followed by a set of skills to be learnt to solve these problems. Quizes are attributed with a fibonacci number, this number signifies the importance of the question for the individual. Quiz questions would be compiled into a test. Based upon the answers to the test questions, attributes would be updated. These attributes are utilized by the patial repition engine to manage the learning process of the member.
